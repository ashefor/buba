BUBA is a bidding and Interactive games platform in Nigeria that allows you to place bids on products for as low as ₦100. There is an interactive gaming platform that allows users to play games such as: Buba Berekete, Buba Wheel, Luckyjaka, Quick play, Daily special.

The options depositing into your Buba wallet includes:

  1. Rolez Microfinance bank
  2. Wema Bank
  3. Flutterwave

Every product has an allocated number of bid slot and a closing bid time, bid slots start from as low as ₦50 depending on the product.

At the close of every deal time, if the total number of bid slot available was bought, we have a completed bid, and one selected winner takes the item. However, if the total number of bid slot sold for that item is not equal to the total number of slots, we have an incomplete bid, hence, one winner is selected through the same process as the completed bid, then 70% of the total bid amount is given to the selected winner.

The more bid slots you buy for an item, the higher your chance of winning!

How to Place a Bid.

  1. Search or select the item you want to view the product description page.
  2. Select the ‘bid now’ option to proceed to the bid checkout page.
  3. On the bid checkout page, provide the number of bid slots you want to buy and proceed.
  4. If you are a new user, you will be asked to create an account and given the option to fund your wallet. However, if you are an existing user, you will be asked to login to finalize your bid.

After the closing time for the bid elapse, your win status is communicated with you via email and SMS to your registered phone number.

Information on Buba Wheel.

The Buba wheel has various options including but not limited to Cash Prizes, amazing products, and unbelievable deals. With one spin, you could become a millionaire, get a chance to try again, or win exciting prizes. Each product spin has different stakes and guarantees you a chance of winning.

How to Spin the Buba Wheel.

  1. Select ‘Games’ from the Menu bar.
  2. Click on ‘Buba Wheel’
  3. Select the product you would like to spin for – you would be able to see the amount to be staked to spin for the product.
  4. Click on ‘Spin’ to spin the wheel.
  5. If you are a new user, you will be asked to create an account and given the option to fund your wallet. However, if you are an existing user, you will be asked to login to spin the wheel.

How to play Daily Special.

  1. Select ‘Games’ from the Menu bar.
  2. Click on ‘Daily Special’
  3. Select 5 numbers from 10 numbers using the hint as a guide.
  4. If you choose the correct set of numbers, the amount staked will be multiplied by 15 to give you your winning lot.
  5. Click on ‘Add to Game Slip’ to create your ticket.
  6. Click on ‘Open Game slip’ to view your ticket and stake the amount you want.
  7. Click on ‘play game’.
  8. If you are a new user, you will be asked to create an account and given the option to fund your wallet. However, if you are an existing user, you will be asked to login to play the game.

How to play Quick Play

  1. Select ‘Games’ from the Menu bar.
  2. Click on ‘Quick Play’
  3. Select one number from each row.
  4. If you choose the correct set of numbers, the amount staked will be multiplied by 120 to give you your winning lot.
  5. Click on ‘Add to Game Slip’ to create your ticket.
  6. Click on ‘Open Game slip’ to view your ticket and stake the amount you want.
  7. Click on ‘play game’.
  8. If you are a new user, you will be asked to create an account and given the option to fund your wallet. However, if you are an existing user, you will be asked to login to play the game.

BUBA BEREKETE is limited to Cash Prizes only. With one spin, you could become a millionaire, win other multiplications of your stakes, or get a chance to try again. You can stake any amount you want to guarantee you a chance of winning.

How to play Buba Berekete

  1. Select ‘Games’ from the Menu bar.
  2. Click on ‘Berekete’
  3. Select the Cash Prize you would like to spin for.
  4. Input the amount you would like to stake.
  5. Click on ‘Spin’ to spin the wheel.
  6. If you are a new user, you will be asked to create an account and given the option to fund your wallet. However, if you are an existing user, you will be asked to login to spin the wheel.
